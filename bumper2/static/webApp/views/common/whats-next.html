  • Booking is scheduled. Car is picked up. What’s next?

    Expect a call: Our customer care team will call you to help you assist with our process .

    Receive crew member information: On the day of pickup, you will get a notification in the app with crew member’s name and phone number who will pick up the car

    Crew will arrive: Crew member will arrive at your location in the scheduled slot.
    1. He will create a job card, check inventory and the working condition of electrical items.

    2. Any personal belongings like Sunglasses, USBs, stereo etc. will be handed over to you.

    3. He will hand over original copy of the job card to you.

    4. He will take photos of the car from all the angles, interior and exterior before leaving with your car.

    5. He will take a copy of RC, insurance and PUC from you..

    6. You will also receive email confirmation immediately with link to inventory in your car , working condition of the electrical items and car photos.

    7. Expect the entire process to take somewhere between 30-45 minutes..

    Inspection: The car will be inspected by our executive at the workshop.
    1. Any change in scope of work, if required, from what was initially requested would be informed to you after inspection.

    2. We will start work on your car only after we receive your approval on the scope of work and delivery time.

    Download app to map progress: You will receive updates on the app at the end of the day everyday at 8 PM. Also, you can check app to know the current status and chat with us.

    Day of Delivery:
    1. Once the car is ready, we do a thorough quality check on the work and the car in general.

    2. You will receive invoice over the email.

    3. We will call you for drop time and location or you can select yourself in the app.

    4. You will get notification in the app with crew member’s name and phone number who will bring the car to you.

    5. Payment: Pay online via app, website, link in SMS/email, card swiping machine and cash.

    6. Once the payment is done, car will be delivered to you.
