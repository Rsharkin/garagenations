Insurance Claim Process
keyboard_arrow_down keyboard_arrow_up

Our workshops are recognized by all the major insurance company, so claiming insurance is easy as it comes. As of now, we provide cashless insurance with Iffco Tokio and Future Generali. For other insurers we provide cash claims.

Here is process -

1. Our team would call to collect the insurance details.

2. We would coordinate with the insurance inspector and schedule the inspection at our workshop.

3. Once approved by the insurance company (full or partial), we will call you to get your approval.

4. Only when you approve it, we will start any work on your car.

5. If any internal damage is identified we would call the inspector to take necessary approvals.

6. You will be kept in the loop during the entire process.

7. If cashless claim, we will send you the invoice with the amount to be paid.

8. If cash claim, we will send the invoice with the full amount to be paid by you. You can get the claimed amount from the insurance company.

Days to Delivery
keyboard_arrow_down keyboard_arrow_up

We understand that your car is an integral part of your life, so we do our best to deliver the car back to you at the earliest. We never compromise on quality. Hence, we don't commit to any delivery date until we inspect the car thoroughly at the workshop. We have created our own benchmarks for quality. Only on passing these checks would the car be cleared for delivery.


For instance -
  • Repairs or Replacement of 1 to 3 Panels will take around 3 Days.
  • Repairs or Replacement of 4 to 6 Panels will take around 5 Days.
  • Repairs or Replacement of 7 to 8 Panels will take around 6 days to a week.
  • Full Body Paint will require a maximum of 10 Days.

Please note that actual delivery date is purely based on the condition of your car and the number of jobs that need to carried out.
