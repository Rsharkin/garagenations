Is there also a dent on the panel

All that appears to be a scratch is not a scratch, at times. Our experts have tried to explain this through the illustration below.

Often there might be a dent as well

Basically, if there is a slightest depression on the surface, it is a dent and would require the surface to be brought back to shape before painting it.

Why does it matter?

Because, dent removal requires additional steps when compared to scratch removal as surface imperfection has to be repaired.
All the scratches on the dented panel are also removed in the dent removal process. Below is the step wise process.
