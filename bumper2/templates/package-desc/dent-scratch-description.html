What it does

Remove Scratches - All scratches are removed and the whole panel is painted.

  • Any number of scratches - same price.
  • Big scratch, small scratch - same price.

Remove Dents and Scratches - All dents and scratches are removed and the whole panel is painted.

  • Any number of dents and scratches - same price.
  • Big dent, small dent - same price.

Panel / part Replacement – Genuine panels and parts are procured from authorized sellers and charged at MRP. It further includes fitting and painting of the new panel.

  • Pricing for panel replacement - Labour + Material charges + Panel MRP.
  • Pricing for part replacement – Fitting charges + Part MRP.

List of panels - Bumpers, Bonnet, All doors, Fenders, Quarter panels, Running boards, Trunk.

List of parts - Headlights, Tail lights, Fog lights, Windshield, Monogram, Indicators, Side view mirror, Grill etc.
