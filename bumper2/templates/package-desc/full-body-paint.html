What it does

It involves repainting of all the panels of your car except the roof. All the dents and scratches are also removed from your car. All panels - bumpers, bonnet, all doors, fenders, quarter panels, running boards, and trunk but the roof are repainted with OEM colour paint.
It has 1 year paint warranty against fading/ peeling/ bubble creation.

Included in the package
Repainting of all the Panels and Parts:
  • Bumpers – front & rear
  • Bonnet
  • All doors – front right & left, rear right & left
  • Fenders – right & left
  • Quarter panels – right & left
  • Running boards – right & left
  • Trunk
  • Petrol cap and if applicable - side view mirror, door handles, upper and lower grill
Not included in the package
  • Cost of procuring the new panels / parts is not included. It is charged over and above the package cost. However, labour charges for replacement are part of the package
Things you should know
  • Initial timelines may vary in case if some part that needs to be replaced has a long procurement cycle.
  • In the following cases, extra charges + time may apply after inspection:
    • Severe damages cases, such as tear, crumpled panel etc.
    • Excessive previous painting work of poor quality.
  • Advance payment might be needed in case of part procurement.
  • Following parts need to be removed at the time of painting. In case, they cannot be fitted back, they will have to be replaced with new ones:
    • Monograms – alignment issue if all letters of logo are separate.
    • Door and Window Beadings - if in bad shape or very old.
    • Bumper beadings

We will let you know of these things after the inspection of the car in the workshop and wait for your approval. We will not start work without your approval.
