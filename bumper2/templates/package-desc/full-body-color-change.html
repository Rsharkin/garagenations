What it does

It involves repainting of all panels of your car. All dents and scratches are also removed from your car. Each and every part of the car where OEM colour paint was applied will be repainted with the new colour including inside sections of door, bonnet etc. Engine compartment is not painted. It has 1 year paint warranty against fading/ peeling/ bubble creation.

Included in the package
  • Each and every part of the car where OEM colour paint was applied will be repainted with the new colour including inside sections of door, bonnet etc.
  • If applicable - side view mirror, door handles, upper and lower grill.
  • 1 year paint warranty against fading/ peeling/ bubble creation. However, this warranty does not hold for any physical damage, accident, use of poor quality material for cleaning or watermarks.
Not included in the package
  • Cost of procuring the new parts is not included. It is charged over and above the package cost. However, labour charges for replacement are part of the package.
Things you should know
  • We will need a letter of authorisation from customer for the change of car colour.
  • Customer is responsible for getting his RC updated.
  • Initial timelines may vary in case some part that needs to be changed has a long procurement cycle.
  • In following cases, extra charges + time may apply after inspection:
    • Severe damages cases, such as tear, crumpled panel etc.
    • Excessive previous painting work of poor quality.
  • Advance payment might be needed in case of part procurement.
